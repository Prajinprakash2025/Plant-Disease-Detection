About The Platform

Designed to make plant disease detection clearer, faster, and easier to act on.

Plant Disease System is a focused web experience for growers, students, and agricultural teams who need a clean workflow around crop diagnosis and account management.

01

Diagnosis-first workflow

The interface is structured around the core plant disease task: upload, inspect, interpret, and respond.

02

Grower-friendly access

Signup, login, profile editing, and dashboard access are all kept simple enough for real users, not only developers.

03

Ready for expansion

This foundation is prepared for image upload modules, disease classification results, recommendations, and report history.
